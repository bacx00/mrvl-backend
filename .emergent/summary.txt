<product_requirements>
The primary goal is to build a Laravel 11 backend for a Marvel Rivals esports platform akin to HLTV.org. It must support multi-role authentication (Admin, Moderator, User), manage comprehensive game data (heroes, maps, modes), and include features like news, forums, and an admin dashboard with real-time statistics.

Key requirements and implemented aspects are:
*   **Core Match Management**: Creation, updates, pausing, and completion of matches, including BO1, BO3, BO5 series with map rotation.
*   **Live Scoring**: Granular player statistics (eliminations, deaths, assists, damage, healing, damage blocked, ultimate usage, objective time, hero played, role played) with real-time updates.
*   **Real-time Viewer Tracking**: Update and retrieve live viewer counts.
*   **Game Data Integration**: Full roster of 39 Marvel Rivals heroes (Vanguard, Duelist, Strategist roles), 11 maps, and 5 game modes (Domination, Convoy, Convergence, Conquest, Doom Match) with accurate, mode-specific timers.
*   **6v6 Match Support**: Expanded player slots (6 per team) with hero composition management.
*   **Match Lifecycle**: Seamless transitions from live to paused, between maps, to completed, and historical archival linked to team/player profiles.
*   **Data Consistency**: Critical need for instant data persistence and reflection from admin updates to public pages for *any* newly created match, across all match formats.
*   **Advanced Features**: Team management, match predictions, VODs, fantasy leagues, and an achievement system.
</product_requirements>
